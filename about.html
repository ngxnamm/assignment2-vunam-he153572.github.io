SUMMARY

Life is a process, not an outcome.

The question “what do you want to be when you grow up” leads people to believe that at some point in the future we will suddenly stop growing, which leads us to believe that life is an outcome. But of course, life isn't an outcome, it's a process, and neither is growth.

Billy Burnet and Dave Evans write that “life design is ultimately a new way of living that transforms the way you see life and the way you live your life. The end result of a well-designed life is a life well lived.”

In other words, when we design our lives, we create our life purpose, give it meaning, and intentionally create the lifestyle we want to lead.
